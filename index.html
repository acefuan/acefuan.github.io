What is Climate Change?

Global climate change refers to the average long-term changes over the entire Earth. These include warming temperatures and changes in precipitation, as well as the effects of Earth’s warming. The climate of the Earth has constantly been changing naturally for centuries now. However, scientists has discovered that due to human activities, the rate of climate change has rapidly increased over the past few years and that the Earth’s average temperature has been increasing much more quickly.

Why should you care about Climate Change?

Not only does the effects of Climate Change put human lives at cost. Other living creatures like animals have also started to die out. The most vulnerable among us – children, the elderly, and the poor – are in danger from increasing heat stress and air pollution. If Climate Change keeps getting worsen, what would be of our future generation? Will there even be a future?
